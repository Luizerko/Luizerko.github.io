About Me

My name is Luis Vitor Zerkowski and I'm a BSc in Computer Science and a MSc in Artificial Intelligence with a few years of hands-on experience building end-to-end machine learning pipelines.

My work focuses in Computer Vision and Interdisciplinary AI, where I enjoy turning innovative and creative ideas into prototypes. As you'll probably be able to tell by my repos, I'm a huge fan of documentation as a form of share lessons learned and as a pedagogical guide to my code. I also love collaborating in diverse and enthusiastic team environments and I'm a bit of math enthusiast. Lastly, I deeply believe in the power of shared knowledge and continuous learning.
